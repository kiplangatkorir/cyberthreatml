Interpretation Guide

How to interpret this report:

  • Feature Importance: Shows which features have the greatest overall impact on model predictions.
  • Sample Prediction: Shows how each feature contributed to a specific prediction:
    • Positive values (green): Increase the likelihood of being classified as a threat
    • Negative values (red): Decrease the likelihood of being classified as a threat
  • Base value: The average model output over the training dataset
  • Final prediction: The probability that the sample is a threat (0-1)
